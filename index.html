IELTS Rosy

Hi, my name is Rosy and I’m a product design manager based in Toronto. I was awarded top writer in design on Medium. Wait! You might be asking yourself “why is a designer writing an IELTS ebook?”

I was born in Vietnam and worked in several countries including Malaysia, Singapore, and Canada. I immigrated to Canada 3 years ago. Everytime I applied for a work permit, It’s been recommended that I take the IELTS test to prove my English level.

I scored 8.0 overall on my General Training IELTS (Listening 8.5, Reading 8.5, Writing 7.0, Speaking 7.5). I also scored Academic IELTS and achieved 7.5 overall. With my previous background as an English tutor, I learned the entire IELTS by myself. I didn’t spend a single dollar going to the IELTS center.

However, a friend of mine from Bangladesh only scored 6.0 on his first attempt. With band 6.0, he didn't meet the English requirements for his Ph.D. He asked me to help him to prepare for the second attempt. Five weeks later, he scored 7.0! That's an increase of 1.0 in 5 weeks! How did we do that?

Excited by the efficient tips we applied, my Bangladeshi friend, my kind Canadian friend and I decided to team up and spent almost 4 years on and off preparing our IELTS ebook on Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking. As writing is the hardest skill to increase your score in, we decided to publish the writing ebook first.

With IELTS Rosy, we aim to improve your IELTS score and teach you the tactics, and techniques you need to achieve writing band 7! It applies to both the academic and general training modules.
